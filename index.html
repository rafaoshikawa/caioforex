About me

----

Caio was born in a small town in Brazil, where he learned the value of hard work early on by closely following his father, a renowned contractor in the region specializing in plaster work. From adolescence, Caio developed a keen interest in the financial world, devouring books on investments and dreaming of the financial freedom they could bring.

When the opportunity to move to Portugal arose, Caio and his family didn't hesitate, seeking new opportunities and a better future. In Portugal, Caio immersed himself in the world of forex, dedicating himself tirelessly to study and practice. With determination, he earned two proprietary trading certificates, standing out as an experienced and successful trader.

Over the years, Caio built his own financial freedom and became a mentor to other aspiring traders, generously sharing his knowledge and methods. Now, he looks to the future with optimism, planning to pass on his legacy to future generations, including his son, ensuring that knowledge and prosperity continue to flourish.
